Welcome to Hands Face Erase
An interactive game based around COVID.
With all the news and new information, we get shown everyday causing confusion we wanted to design an interactive and fun way to learn about covid-19 helping to keep everyone safe, either at home or while you wait in those essential queues why not give Hands Face Erase a go.
How to play:
  • Ensure you are always two meters away from others while playing Hands Face Erase.

  • FACE- if anyone comes in front of your camera without wearing a face mask throw one on their face in order to help everyone be protected.
  • ERASE- use your Anti-bacterial spray to clean the area of COVID spores and stop them from touching you and others.
